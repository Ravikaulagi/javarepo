WHAT DO YOU KNOW ABOUT KADAMBARI

KadambariKādambari is a romantic novel it Sanskrit.It was substantially composed by Bāṇabhaṭṭa in the first half of the century CE, who did not survive to see it through completion. novel was completed by Banabhatta's son Bhushanabhatta, according to the plan laid out by his late father. It is conventionally divided into Purvabhaga (earlier part) written by Banabhatta, and Uttarabhaga (latter part) by Bhushanabhatta
